## Company Overview

**ChiroHD** is a specialty-built chiropractic EHR (Electronic Health Record) platform serving 5,000+ chiropractic practices. The company is PE-backed by Mainsail Partners, with an estimated valuation around $10M.

**Mission:** Provide chiropractors with purpose-built software that understands their specialty workflows — not a generic EHR adapted for chiropractic use.

## Product

ChiroHD's core platform includes:

- **SOAP Notes** — Clinical documentation tailored to chiropractic workflows
- **Scheduling** — Appointment management for chiropractic practices
- **Billing & Claims** — Insurance billing, claims management, revenue cycle
- **Patient Engagement** — Patient communication and retention tools
- **Practice Management** — Operational tools for running a chiropractic practice

### Differentiator

ChiroHD is **specialty-built**, not a generic EHR with chiropractic bolted on. This matters because chiropractic workflows (high-frequency visits, specific documentation patterns, adjustment-focused SOAP notes) don't fit well into general-purpose EHR templates.

- Window of opportunity: specialty + AI = defensible position if moved on quickly
