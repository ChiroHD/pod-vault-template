## Alina pod

### Eshin Griffith — Alina pod engineering lead

Runs the Alina pod largely autonomously. Drives product decisions in addition to engineering — the pod operates "like a factory" continuously shipping. James Hendrie's read: he does a good job and is intellectually serious, but he is severely overbooked (estimated ~75% over capacity at the time of handoff, ~8 hours/week of deep work).

Organizationally a "half-breed" — came over from the ChiroHD side when Alina was moved into SKED, attends SKED standups and is treated as part of the team, but his identity and authority sit in a middle ground. **Uses JIRA personally** while mirroring tickets to GitLab as a gesture to the rest of SKED. Expect to engage him in both tools.

Handle with care: he was worried about job security during the handoff window. Over-asserting PM authority will cause friction; under-asserting leaves you without visibility. Ask him to build/refresh lower environments rather than learning the pipeline yourself.
